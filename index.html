Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1973

The St. Xavier's Church is located in the centre of Nuwara Eliya and one of the oldest church in Sri Lanka.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The Central Library of the school has a collection of thousands of books, old and rare books, reference books, journals, educational CDs/DVDs and a large number of e-books, e-journals and other resources. Web OPAC system is installed in the library. The library has a subscription of numerous periodicals, including both journals and magazines dailies. The library also has Institutional Membership with British Council Library and American Library.





School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The auditorium of the school has a large seating capacity. It is an ideal place to organise celebrations, discussions, cultural events and other events.
